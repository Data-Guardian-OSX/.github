## 🖌 Overview  
Data Guardian Advanced is a secure personal data manager that protects your most sensitive information behind strong AES encryption. Passwords, PINs, credit cards, software licenses, and private notes all live in one encrypted vault.

The encryption happens entirely on your device. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, and no cloud account is required. Your information stays where it belongs — with you.

The interface is clear and efficient. Create categories, add custom fields, search across all records instantly, and access exactly what you need without friction.

Data Guardian Advanced is designed with security-conscious users in mind. The vault locks automatically after inactivity, and access is protected by your master password with optional biometric support.

For anyone who takes privacy seriously, Data Guardian Advanced is the personal data vault that gives you confidence your information is safe.
